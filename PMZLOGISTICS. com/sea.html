🌊 Sea Freight Services

When time allows but cost efficiency is crucial, sea freight is the smartest way to move your cargo across continents. At Pmzlogistics, we provide comprehensive sea freight solutions tailored to meet your unique logistics needs—whether you're shipping full container loads (FCL), less than container loads (LCL), or specialized cargo.

Why Choose Us for Sea Freight?

  • Global Network: With partnerships across major international ports, we ensure seamless movement of your goods from origin to destination—no matter the country or continent.
  • Flexible Container Options: From 20ft to 40ft containers, open-top to flat-rack, we offer flexible shipping configurations to suit all cargo types, including oversized and hazardous goods.
  • Customs Clearance & Documentation: Our experienced team handles all the paperwork, customs procedures, and compliance regulations to ensure your cargo sails smoothly through borders.
  • Real-Time Tracking: Know where your shipment is at any time with our advanced cargo tracking system. Transparency and peace of mind come standard.
  • Competitive Rates: Thanks to our strategic carrier relationships and consolidated volumes, we offer highly competitive rates without compromising on service quality.
